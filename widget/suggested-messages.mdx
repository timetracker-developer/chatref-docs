# Suggested Messages

**Suggested messages** are pre-written questions or prompts that appear as clickable buttons in your chat widget. They help visitors start a conversation by offering common topics they can ask about with a single click.

## Why Use Suggested Messages

- **Lower the barrier** — Visitors don't have to think of what to type; they can just click
- **Guide conversations** — Steer visitors toward topics your chatbot handles best
- **Improve engagement** — Clickable options encourage more visitors to interact with your chatbot
- **Showcase capabilities** — Show visitors what your chatbot can help with

## How to Set Up Suggested Messages

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Suggested Messages">
    Open your agent's dashboard. The suggested messages settings can be found in the agent settings area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a message">
    Click **Add** to create a new suggested message. Type the text that will appear on the button (e.g., "What are your business hours?" or "Tell me about your products").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add more messages">
    Repeat to add as many suggested messages as you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reorder with drag and drop">
    Drag and drop messages to change their display order. The first message in the list appears first in the widget.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save** to apply.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Suggested Messages

- **Edit** — Click on any existing message to change its text
- **Delete** — Remove messages you no longer want to show
- **Reorder** — Drag and drop to change the order

## Tips for Effective Suggested Messages

<Tip>
- **Keep them short** — Aim for 3-7 words per message
- **Use natural language** — Write them as real questions visitors would ask
- **Cover your top topics** — Include your most common or important questions
- **Limit the number** — 3-5 suggested messages is usually ideal; too many can be overwhelming
- **Match your audience** — Use language your visitors would naturally use
</Tip>

## Examples

Here are some suggested message ideas for different types of businesses:

**E-commerce store:**
- "What's your return policy?"
- "Where's my order?"
- "What sizes are available?"

**SaaS product:**
- "How does pricing work?"
- "Can I get a free trial?"
- "How do I get started?"

**Service business:**
- "What services do you offer?"
- "How do I book an appointment?"
- "What are your hours?"
