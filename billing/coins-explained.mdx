# Coins Explained

Chatref uses a **coin system** to measure and manage your chatbot's usage. Coins are the credits that power your chatbot's conversations. Every time your chatbot responds to a visitor, it uses a small number of coins.

## What Are Coins?

Coins are usage credits included with your Chatref plan. Think of them like a prepaid balance — each month, your plan gives you a set number of coins, and your chatbot uses them as it has conversations with visitors.

## How Many Coins Do I Get?

The number of coins you receive each month depends on your plan:

| Plan | Coins Per Month |
|------|----------------|
| **Free** | 100 |
| **Basic** | 500 |
| **Plus** | 3,000 |
| **Pro** | 10,000 |

When you first create a Chatref account, you start on the **Free plan** with **100 coins** — enough to test your chatbot and see how it works.

## How Are Coins Spent?

Your chatbot uses coins every time it responds to a visitor's message. The number of coins per response depends on the length and complexity of the conversation:

- **Short, simple exchanges** — Typically cost **1-2 coins** per response. For example, a visitor asking "What are your hours?" and getting a brief answer.
- **Medium conversations** — Usually cost **2-3 coins** per response. For example, a product question that requires a detailed explanation.
- **Long, detailed responses** — May cost **3-5 coins** per response. For example, a comprehensive comparison of multiple products or a step-by-step guide.

### Example: A Typical Conversation

Here's what a typical conversation might cost:

| Message | Coin Cost |
|---------|-----------|
| Visitor asks: "What's your return policy?" | — |
| Chatbot responds with the return policy | ~1 coin |
| Visitor asks: "How do I start a return?" | — |
| Chatbot explains the step-by-step process | ~2 coins |
| Visitor asks: "Can I return a sale item?" | — |
| Chatbot provides the specific policy for sale items | ~1 coin |
| **Total for this conversation** | **~4 coins** |

This means on the Free plan (100 coins), you could have roughly **25-50 conversations** per month, depending on length.

<Info>
Only your chatbot's responses cost coins. Visitor messages don't use any coins.
</Info>

## Monthly Coin Refresh

Your coins **reset at the beginning of each billing cycle**. This means:

- On the 1st of each billing period, your coin balance is refreshed to your plan's full allocation
- Unused coins from the previous month **do not carry over** — they reset
- Your billing cycle starts from the date you subscribed or last renewed

### Example
If you're on the Basic plan (500 coins/month) and you used 300 coins this month, your balance resets to 500 at the start of your next billing period. The 200 unused coins don't roll over.

## What Happens When Coins Run Out?

If your coin balance reaches zero before your next refresh:

- Your chatbot will **stop responding** to new visitor messages
- Existing conversations will show that the chatbot is currently unavailable
- Your chatbot will **automatically resume** when your coins refresh at the next billing cycle

To avoid running out mid-month, you can:

1. **Upgrade your plan** — Move to a higher plan for more coins
2. **Monitor your usage** — Keep an eye on your remaining balance in the billing dashboard

## Low Balance Warnings

Chatref will send you an **email notification** when your coin balance drops to **20% or less** of your monthly allocation. This gives you time to either:

- Conserve usage by limiting chatbot activity
- Upgrade to a plan with more coins

You'll only receive one low-balance email per billing period, so you won't be spammed with alerts.

## Coin Transaction Types

Your coin activity is tracked through different transaction types:

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Deduction** | Coins used for chatbot responses (the most common type) |
| **Purchase** | Coins added when you subscribe or upgrade your plan |
| **Refund** | Coins returned if there's a billing adjustment |
| **Bonus** | Promotional coins given during special offers |
| **Gift** | Coins gifted by the Chatref team |
| **Adjustment** | Manual corrections to your coin balance |

## Tips to Optimize Your Coin Usage

<Tip>
1. **Write a focused system prompt** — A clear system prompt helps your chatbot give concise, relevant answers instead of long-winded responses
2. **Adjust the temperature** — Lower temperature values (0.3-0.5) tend to produce shorter, more focused responses
3. **Use suggested messages** — Guide visitors toward topics your chatbot handles efficiently
4. **Keep your knowledge base lean** — Well-organized, relevant content leads to more accurate (and shorter) responses
5. **Monitor conversations** — Review your Inbox to identify any repetitive or unnecessarily long responses, and adjust your training data accordingly
</Tip>

## Checking Your Balance

You can check your current coin balance and usage at any time from the **Billing** section of your dashboard. It shows:

- Your current remaining coins
- Total coins allocated for this period
- When your next refresh date is
