# Managing Your Subscription

Everything related to your billing and subscription can be managed from the **Billing** section in your dashboard sidebar.

## Viewing Your Current Plan

The Billing page shows you:

- **Your current plan** and its features
- **Coin balance** — How many coins you have remaining this period
- **Coins allocated** — Your total monthly coin allocation
- **Next billing date** — When your subscription renews and coins refresh

## Upgrading Your Plan

To upgrade to a higher plan:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Click **Billing** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Upgrade or Change Plan">
    You'll see the available plans with their features and pricing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your new plan">
    Choose the plan you want and your preferred billing frequency (monthly or annual).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and pay">
    Enter or confirm your payment method and complete the upgrade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When you upgrade:
- Your new plan takes effect **immediately**
- You receive the new plan's full coin allocation right away
- You're charged the prorated difference for the current billing period

## Downgrading Your Plan

You can downgrade to a lower plan at any time:

1. Go to **Billing** and click **Change Plan**
2. Select the lower plan
3. Confirm the downgrade

When you downgrade:
- The change takes effect at the **end of your current billing period**
- You keep your current plan's features and coins until then
- At the next billing cycle, you'll switch to the new plan with its coin allocation

## Switching Between Monthly and Annual

You can switch between monthly and annual billing at any time from the Billing page. Annual billing saves you money — see the [Plans & Pricing](/billing/plans-and-pricing) page for the exact savings.

## Updating Your Payment Method

To update your credit card or payment method:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Click **Billing** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Update Payment Method">
    You'll be directed to a secure page to enter your new payment details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your new method">
    Enter your new card details and save. Future charges will use this payment method.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Billing History

Your billing page shows a history of past payments and transactions, including:

- Subscription charges
- Plan changes
- Payment method updates

## Cancelling Your Subscription

If you need to cancel your subscription:

1. Go to **Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm the cancellation

When you cancel:
- You keep access to your current plan until the end of the billing period
- After the period ends, your account reverts to the **Free plan** (100 coins/month)
- Your chatbots, knowledge base, and settings are **preserved** — they're not deleted
- You can resubscribe at any time to restore your previous plan level

<Info>
Cancelling doesn't delete your account or data. Your chatbots will continue to work on the Free plan's 100 coins per month, and you can upgrade again whenever you're ready.
</Info>

## Common Questions

### What happens if my payment fails?
If a payment can't be processed, you'll be notified by email. Your plan continues for a short grace period while you update your payment method.

### Can I get a refund?
Contact Chatref support for refund requests. Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis.

### Do unused coins carry over?
No. Coins reset at the beginning of each billing period. Unused coins do not roll over to the next month.

### Can I have multiple subscriptions?
Each workspace has one subscription. If you need separate billing for different projects, create separate workspaces.
