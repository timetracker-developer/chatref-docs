## Free Plan

**Best for:** Trying out Chatref and small personal projects.

The Free plan gives you 100 coins per month — enough to test your chatbot and have a handful of conversations. It includes all core features: unlimited chatbots, knowledge base training, playground testing, and conversation history.

## Basic Plan — $12/month

**Best for:** Small businesses and personal websites with moderate traffic.

With 500 coins per month, the Basic plan supports roughly **125-250 conversations** monthly. You get all the features of the Free plan with a higher usage allowance.

## Plus Plan — $39/month

**Best for:** Growing businesses that want full branding control and multiple chatbots.

The Plus plan is our **most popular choice**. With 3,000 coins per month (roughly **750-1,500 conversations**), it's great for businesses with steady website traffic. Plus unlocks premium features:

- **Custom logo** — Replace Chatref branding with your own
- **Custom footer** — Add your own footer text
- **Button label** — Add text to the chat bubble
- **Multi-agent routing** — Show different chatbots on different pages
- **Display rules** — URL-based routing for multi-agent setups

## Pro Plan — $99/month

**Best for:** High-traffic websites and businesses with heavy chatbot usage.

With 10,000 coins per month (roughly **2,500-5,000 conversations**), the Pro plan is built for businesses that rely heavily on chatbot support. It includes all Plus features with the highest coin allocation.

## Which Plan Is Right for You?

Ask yourself these questions:

1. **How much traffic does your website get?** Higher traffic = more conversations = more coins needed
2. **How important is branding?** If you want to remove Chatref branding and use your own logo, you need Plus or Pro
3. **Do you need multiple chatbots on different pages?** Multi-agent routing requires Plus or Pro
4. **How long are typical conversations?** Longer conversations use more coins per session

<Tip>
Start with the Free plan to test your chatbot, then upgrade when you're ready. You can always change your plan as your needs grow.
</Tip>

## How to Subscribe

1. Go to **Billing** in your dashboard sidebar
2. Click **Upgrade** or **Change Plan**
3. Select your desired plan and billing frequency (monthly or annual)
4. Enter your payment information
5. Your new plan activates immediately with a fresh coin allocation

For details on managing your subscription, see [Managing Your Subscription](/billing/managing-your-subscription).
