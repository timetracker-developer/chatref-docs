# Welcome to Chatref

Chatref is an AI-powered chatbot platform that lets you create intelligent chatbots for your website. Train them on your own content — documents, web pages, or custom text — and your chatbot will answer your visitors' questions accurately using your information.

## How It Works

Getting started with Chatref takes just a few minutes:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Agent">
    Set up a new AI chatbot and give it a name, welcome message, and personality through a system prompt.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your Knowledge">
    Upload PDFs, crawl your website, or paste text directly. Chatref processes your content so the chatbot can reference it in conversations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test in the Playground">
    Try out your chatbot in a live testing environment. Tweak settings until you're happy with the responses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Embed on Your Website">
    Copy a simple code snippet and paste it into your website. Your chatbot is now live and ready to help visitors.
  </Step>
</Steps>

- **Support teams** looking to automate common questions and reduce ticket volume
- **E-commerce stores** that need a product assistant to help shoppers
- **Content creators** who want to make their knowledge base interactive
- **Agencies** managing chatbots for multiple clients
